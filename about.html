The Story

I built Self-MARIA because the interview room is broken.

After going through hundreds of interviews myself and consulting with hiring managers and HR leaders, I began to see patterns, which led me to develop a clear, repeatable approach - one that helped me regain control of the interview.

When I shared it with colleagues and friends, their feedback was immediate and powerful. They felt calmer, more confident, and more in control.

That is when I realized this was not just helpful; it was necessary.


Interviews are performance environments. And performance can be trained.


Self-MARIA is a system designed to help you walk into interviews with intention. When you have nailed these five elements, you stop guessing and panicking.

The Course combines:

• behavioral interview framework
• hiring-manager perspective
• repeatable interview preparation system

Most candidates walk into interviews hoping their experience speaks for itself. It rarely does.

Self-MARIA helps you communicate it properly.
